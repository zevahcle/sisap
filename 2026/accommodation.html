Scam Alert – Exploreera Reservation Emails

Please note that last year, the SISAP conference experienced issues with a company operating under the name Exploreera (using the email address reservation.nl@exploreera.info), which contacted attendees of SISAP 2025 claiming to offer accommodation services.

Please be aware that SISAP 2026 is not associated with this company in any way, and it is not authorised to act on our behalf.
