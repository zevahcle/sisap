Accommodation

SISAP 2026 will be held at the Faculty of Informatics, Masaryk University, Brno, Czech Republic, Botanicka 68a, 602 00 Brno, Czech Republic ( website , map ).

The conference organisers have reserved the following hotel rooms at discounted rates. The prices include breakfasts. If you wish to book one of these rooms, use the booking code that you should receive after completing your conference registration. The codes will expire on 10 September 2026 (AoE).

Scam Alert – Exploreera Reservation Emails

Please note that last year, the SISAP conference experienced issues with a company operating under the name Exploreera (using the email address reservation.nl@exploreera.info), which contacted attendees of SISAP 2025 claiming to offer accommodation services. Please be aware that SISAP 2026 is not associated with this company in any way, and it is not authorised to act on our behalf.
