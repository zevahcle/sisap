Registration

We have three types of registrations:

  • Regular participant (300 €): includes admission to conference halls, conference bag, proceedings, coffee breaks, lunches, conference dinner, social event.
  • Student participant (250 €): same as regular registration, but limited to full-time students (including PhD students); a document confirming student status will be required at the registration desk.
  • Accompanying person (120 €): non-conference participant (e.g., friend, partner). Includes conference dinner and social event.

Registration fees should be paid through paypal. Please use the appropriate button below. For authors: please, include your paper title in the description field of the paypal page.
