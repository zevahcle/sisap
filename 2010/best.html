Best papers

The editors of special issue of Journal of Discrete Algorithms (and SISAP 2010 co-chairs) selected the four best papers that were invited to the mentioned journal issue. The selected papers are (alphabetically order by the authors' name):

  • Ole Edsberg and Magnus Lie Hetland: Indexing inexact proximity search with distance regression in pivot space
  • Rui Mao, Willard Miranker and Daniel Miranker: Dimension Reduction for Distance-Based Indexing
  • Jiri Novak, Tomas Skopal, David Hoksza and Jakub Lokoc: Improving the Similarity Search of Tandem Mass Spectra using Metric Access Methods
  • Vladimir Pestov: Indexability, Concentration, and VC Theory

The decision was based primarily on the ranks in reviews of the original SISAP submissions, on the oral presentation and on topic coverage.
