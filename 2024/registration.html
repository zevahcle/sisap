SISAP 2024 - Registration

Registration to SISAP 2024 provides access to all three days of the conference, including breakfast, lunch, coffee breaks, the conference banquet, and the Newport social outing. Please note that each accepted paper is expected to have one author with a full registration, i.e., either a Regular registration or a Student registratrion.
 
One-day registration provides access to a single day of the conference, perfect for local people who would like to attend select presentations or keynote. The admission includes the meals and coffee breaks, but does not provide access to the conference banquet or the Newport outing. The specific day must be chosen at the time of purchase, so please refer to the conference program. A person is limited to only a single one-day registration, so those who would like to attend multiple days must purchase a full registration.
 
The next table shows the registration fees and the deadlines for registration:
 
Type Fee Deadline
Regular registration $575 September 13th, 2024 October 21st, 2024
Student registration $450 September 13th, 2024 October 21st, 2024
Regular one-day registration $100 November 6th, 2024
Student one-day registration $50 November 6th, 2024
 
You may register for the conference through the registration gateway.
 
Those purchasing a full registration will be asked questions about their attendance to WaterFire and the Newport Outing. Please view the descriptions of these events on the venue page.
