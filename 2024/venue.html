SISAP 2024
November 4th-6th
Providence, RI, USA

Conference Venue

SISAP 2024 will be held at Brown University in Providence, RI, USA. The university was founded in 1764, making it one of the oldest in the United States. The conference itself will be held in the Crystal Room in Brown Alumnae hall, Please note that the conference will be held in the beginning of November, just before the start of the winter months in New England. The temperature can be a bit cold, typically ranging from 40-60 °F (4-15 °C), with the possibility for snow. Please dress accordingly.

WaterFire: November 2nd

WaterFire in Providence is an outdoor art experience that sets the city’s rivers alight. First created by local artist Barnaby Evans in 1994, WaterFire is a free public art installation that is held November - May, once or twice a month. We invite conference participants to arrive early to experience WaterFire and the vibrant energy of the city.
 

Newport Outing: November 3rd

Despite Rhode Island being the smallest state in US, it is known as the "Ocean State" for its 400 miles (643 km) of coastline. Newport, Rhode Island is one of the most scenic cities in Rhode Island, known for its stunning ocean views and rich history. The city was a summer home to many of America’s wealthiest families during the late 19th and early 20th centuries, and now holds a collection of breath-taking, well-maintained mansions. Running alongside the Breaker's Mansion is the well-known Newport Cliff Walk, a beautiful 3.5 mile (5.6 km) walking trail that stretches along the shoreline of Newport.
 
On Sunday, November 3rd, the day before the conference begins, there will be a bus to take people to Newport for the day. The trip will give people a few hours to explore Newport, experience local cuisine, and walk the Newport Cliff Walk. The bus will also bring people to a walking tour of the Breaker's Mansions. The Newport Outing is automatically included with the purchase of a full registration.
