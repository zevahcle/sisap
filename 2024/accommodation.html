Accommodation

Hotel

SISAP 2024 has a room block reserved at The Hampton Inn & Suites in Downtown Providence. The hotel is a 20 minute walk from the conference venue.
 
 
The booking at The Hampton Inn & Suites includes:
  • Complimentary hotel shuttle around downtown Providence and Brown University. This can take participants to/from the conference venue.
  • Full hot complimentary breakfast
  • Access to the fitness center
The hotel shuttle is available to take guests around the Downtown Providence area and Brown University. The shuttle can be contacted by calling +1(774)301-2614 or can be scheduled be contacting the hotel at +1(401)608-3500.

The discounted rate is $149/night (before taxes), except for Saturday Nov. 2nd which is $199/night. This booking is available until October 18, 2024. You can book your room here. After clicking "Book a Room", be sure to click "Edit Stay" (top right) to book only for the desired nights.

Airports

The closest airport to the conference venue is T.F. Green Airport (PVD) located in Warwick, RI. Since T.F. Green is a small airport, most international flights will likely come to Logan Airport (BOS) in Boston, MA.

We recommend that participants use Uber to travel to/from the airport, it will be the most convenient form of transportation.

T.F. Green Airport: Guests at the hotel may arrange for the hotel shuttle to pick them up at T.F. Green Airport for a fee of $15.00 each way. To schedule a pickup, please contact the hotel at +1 (401) 608-3500. The hotel shuttle cannot pickup participants at Logan Airport.

Logan Airport: The most direct route to get from Logan Airport to Providence is by Uber, but it is possible to get there by train. The train does not directly go to Logan Airport, so you must first get to train station (either Backbay station or South station). You can get there by taxi, Uber, or the Silver Line (bus). Once at the train station, you can take the Commuter Rail (train) to the Providence Train Station. The Providence Train Station is a 10 minute walk from the hotel, or guests may schedule the hotel shuttle to pick them up (at no charge).
