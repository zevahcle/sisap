Accomodation

SISAP 2025 will be held at Nauthóll, next to Reykjavík University. There are several accommodation options available within walking distance or a short bus/taxi ride from the venue.


  • Icelandair Hotel Reykjavik Natura
    is the closest accommodation to the Conference Venue. Located by the domestic airport and within 15 minutes' walk from the venue.
    A limited number of rooms are available through the SISAP 2025 registration system. Alternatively, you can book directly via the hotel website with a 10% discount using this booking link .
