Accomodation

SISAP 2025 will be held at Nauthóll, next to Reykjavík University. There are several accommodation options available within walking distance or a short bus/taxi ride from the venue.


  • Hotel Leifur Eiríksson (located in the city centre).
    A limited number of rooms have been prebooked for SISAP attendees for the period 30 September – 4 October. For additional nights, please contact info@komum.is.
    These rooms can be booked through the SISAP 2025 registration system.
  • Icelandair Hotel Reykjavik Natura
    This hotel is very close to the conference venue, located by the domestic airport and within 15 minutes' walk from Nauthóll.
    You can book directly via the hotel website with a 10% discount using this booking link .
