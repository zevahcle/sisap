Closing and Awards

chair: Fabrizio Falchi (ISTI-CNR)

Presentations (live presentation, no pre-recorded video):

  • Short papers: 10' + 5' q&a
  • Full papers: 20' + 5' q&a
  • Doctoral symposium papers: 30' + 10' q&a

Poster session:

  • A 1x2m board will be available for every presenter (except Doctoral Symposium) to hang a printed poster (A1 max size, vertical, clips will be available onsite)
  • Short paper presenters are expected to provide additional details (e.g., experiments, ongoing research)
  • Full paper presenters are expected to provide a teaser for their presentation
  • Demo paper presenters will also have a table and a nearby plug for their laptop
