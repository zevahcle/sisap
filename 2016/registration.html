Registration at SISAP 2016 includes the conference proceedings, meals and coffee breaks during the conference, and the conference banquet.

The following table shows the base registration fees at SISAP 2016 broken down by registration type and applicable deadlines.

Type Deadline Base Fee
Regular Student
Early Registration† August 25th, 2016 JPY 65,000 JPY 45,000
Late Registration October 21st, 2016 JPY 70,000 JPY 50,000
Additional Banquet Tickets JPY 10,000

†Note that at least one early registration is required for each accepted paper.
