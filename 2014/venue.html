Conference venue

SISAP 2014 will be held at Barceló Grand Faro Los Cabos hotel.

Address: Blvd. San Jose S/N, Sección Hotelera 1 | Zona FONATUR C.P. 23400 | San Jose Del Cabo, B.C.S. México
Telephone number: +52 (624) 14 292 92
Email: grandfaroloscabos@barcelo.com

The Barceló Grand Faro Los Cabos hotel is situated right on the beach-front of the Sea of Cortés. It is in a privileged location given that the city centre of San José is just 2.5 km and the airport only 19 km away from the hotel. It is also close to the Marina Puerto Los Cabos.
