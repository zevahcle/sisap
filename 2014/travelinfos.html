Dear SISAP 2014 attendee,

Probably some of you have watched in the News about the Odile hurricane that has damaged the Baja California and Cabo San Lucas. We´d like to assure you all that the Conference venue at the Barceló Grand Faro Los Cabos hotel will be ready for holding the SISAP 2014 Conference as expected.

Fortunately, our time frame allows us to keep the SISAP 2014 conference on schedule.

We wish to meet you all at Los Cabos soon!

The organization team of SISAP 2014
