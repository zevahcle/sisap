Travel Information

Airport Access

From Franz-Josef-Strauß Airport (MUC) take either the suburban train line S8 (directed to Herrsching) or S1 (directed to Ostbahnhof) and exit at Isartorplatz. Ascend to ground level and walk to the tram station. Take the Tram line 18 (directed to Effnerplatz) and exit at Tivolistraße.

Fares:

Single Ticket (4 zones) 11,20 €
Single Day-Ticket (entire network) 12,80 €
Group Adult Day-Ticket (entire network) 23,90 €

Long Distance Train Access

From Munich Central Station (München Hauptbahnhof) take the subway U5 (directed to Neuperlach Süd) and exit at Lehel. Ascend to ground level and walk to the tram station. Take the Tram line 18 (directed to Effnerplatz) and exit at Tivolistraße.

Fares:

Single Ticket (1 zone) 2,70 €
Single Day-Ticket (inner disctrict/white zone) 6,60 €
Group Adult Day-Ticket (inner district/white zone) 12,60 €

Visa Information

Non-EU-citizens from a variety of countries need a visa to enter Germany. Information on this topic can be found on the Federal Foreign Office website. Information on which countries need a visa to enter Germany can be found here.

If you need a support letter for funding, visa, etc. (e.g. letter of acceptance or visa support letter), please contact us by mail to the Local Arrangement Chair. Attendees must be registered in order to request a visa support letter. For your invitation letter request, please include the following information to your e-mail:

  • date of birth
  • your passport/ID number
  • confirmation number
  • postal address
