SISAP 2023
Oct 9-11, A Coruña, Spain

Accommodation

 
SISAP 2023 will be held at Hotel Riazor (www.riazorhotel.com). The following table shows the room rates (breakfast included) for conference attendants:
 
Item Cost
Double room (for single or double use) 65.97 € per room/night
Breakfast (buffet) 12 € per person/day
Supplement for sea views 15 € per room/night
 
You can directly book your room at Hotel Riazor.
 
NOTICE that to enjoy the special rate for participants, you must book your accommodation by contacting the hotel by mail at reservas@riazorhotel.com and specifying that you are attending the SISAP 2023 conference, organized by Universidade da Coruña.
 
If you have any doubt or problem during the process, please contact us at oscar.pedriera@udc.es.
