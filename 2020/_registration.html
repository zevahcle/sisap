Registration

Registration at SISAP 2015 includes the conference proceedings, meals and coffee breaks during the conference, the conference banquet, and the social events.

The next table shows the registration fees and the deadlines for early and late registration at SISAP 2015:

Type Fee Deadline
Early Bird and Author Registration* £400.- 9th August
Early Bird Student Registration £350.- 9th August
Late Registration £450.- 11th October

*At least one non-student registration is required for each accepted paper by August 9th.

Please, proceed to online registration and send us a copy of the registration receipt and the corresponding paper id by email to sisap2015@isti.cnr.it.

If you have any doubt or problem during the process, please contact us at richard.connor@strath.ac.uk.
